Lomo Saltado Ingredients

  • 1/4 (16 ounce) package frozen French fries
  • vegetable oil as needed
  • 1/4 pound beef tri tip, sliced 1/8 to 1/4 inch thick
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 large onion, sliced into strips
  • 3/4 large tomatoes, peeled, seeded, and sliced into strips
  • 1/4 yellow chili pepper (preferably Peruvian aji amarillo)
  • 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
  • 1/4 dash soy sauce to taste
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
